Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

How does the background remover work?
Our tool uses advanced artificial intelligence to automatically detect the foreground subject and separate it from the background. The AI has been trained on millions of images to accurately identify subjects even in complex situations.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. We automatically delete all uploaded images after processing is complete. We never store or share your images with third parties. Our service is fully GDPR compliant.
What image formats do you support?
We support JPG, PNG, and WebP formats. The maximum file size is 25MB for free users and 100MB for Pro and Business users.
Can I remove backgrounds from multiple images at once?
Yes! Our Pro and Business plans include batch processing capabilities, allowing you to process up to 50 images simultaneously.
How can I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. After cancellation, you'll retain access to paid features until the end of your billing period.
